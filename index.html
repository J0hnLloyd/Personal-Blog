What is... Narezano?

I go by the username 'Narezano'. The name comes from the endings of my surname and middle name syllables, and it holds a lot of meaning for me. I created it as a way to honor both sides of my family, blending them into something unique that represents who I am.

My families mean the world to me, and they’re a big part of why I pursue my passions and work toward my goals. Everything I do is rooted in the love and support they’ve given me, and Narezano is my way of carrying that with me.
